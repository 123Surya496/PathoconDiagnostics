Care tailored to your unique needs.

Diagnostic Services
  • General Medicine: Our experienced physicians provide comprehensive care for both acute and chronic illnesses, focusing on preventive healthcare, early diagnosis, and managing lifestyle-related conditions.
  • Cardiology: We offer advanced diagnostics and treatment for heart-related conditions, including routine check-ups, ECG, echocardiography, and preventive screenings.
  • Diabetology: Specialized care for diabetes management with customized treatment plans, nutritional advice, and continuous glucose monitoring.
  • Endocrinology: We provide treatments for hormonal imbalances such as thyroid disorders, adrenal dysfunction, and metabolic issues, helping to restore balance and improve quality of life.
  • Pulmonology: Comprehensive respiratory care for conditions like asthma, COPD, and lung infections, utilizing advanced tools such as spirometry and imaging for precise care.
Specialized Medical Services
  • Nephrology: Expert care for kidney diseases, from chronic conditions to acute infections. Our nephrology team emphasizes early diagnosis and patient education.
  • Urology: Comprehensive diagnostics and treatments for urinary tract disorders, prostate health, and male reproductive issues, using advanced technology for effective care.
  • Orthopaedics: Specialized care for bone, joint, and muscle disorders, ranging from fractures to joint replacement surgeries and sports injury care.
  • Psychiatry: Confidential mental health services for conditions like anxiety, depression, and stress management, offering therapy and medication management tailored to individual needs.
  • Physiotherapy: Personalized rehabilitation programs to improve mobility, reduce pain, and aid in post-injury recovery, post-surgical rehabilitation, and chronic pain management.
Laboratory and Pathology Services
  • Haematology: Precise testing and management of blood disorders like anemia, clotting issues, and leukemia.
  • Biochemistry: Testing for metabolic conditions such as diabetes, liver disorders, and kidney function.
  • Microbiology: Pathogen identification for diagnosing infections caused by bacteria, viruses, and fungi.
  • Serology: Testing for antibodies and antigens to diagnose infections and assess immune response.
  • Histopathology: Examination of tissue samples for early diagnosis of cancers and other diseases.
  • Cytology: Analysis of cells to detect abnormalities, including early signs of cancer.
  • Clinical Pathology: Comprehensive diagnostic services for blood, urine, and other fluids to aid diagnosis.
  • Hormones & Immunoassays: Specialized tests for hormonal and immune disorders like thyroid conditions and infertility.
Imaging and Radiology Services
  • Computerized Radiography (X-Ray): High-resolution imaging to diagnose fractures, joint problems, and soft tissue conditions.
  • Doppler Studies: Non-invasive tests to assess blood flow and detect vascular issues like blockages and varicose veins.
  • Ultrasound Studies: Imaging to diagnose internal organ conditions, monitor pregnancy, and detect abnormalities.
  • Echocardiography: Heart imaging to assess structure, function, and blood flow for diagnosing heart conditions.
  • ECG & TMT: Tests to diagnose arrhythmias, ischemia, and cardiac stress, ensuring early intervention and treatment.
