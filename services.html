Diagnostic Services

General Medicine: Comprehensive care for acute and chronic illnesses.
Cardiology: Advanced diagnostics and treatment for heart-related conditions.
Diabetology: Specialized care for diabetes management.
Endocrinology: Diagnostics for hormonal imbalances.

Specialized Medical Services

Nephrology: Expert care for kidney diseases.
Urology: Treatment for urinary tract and male reproductive health.
