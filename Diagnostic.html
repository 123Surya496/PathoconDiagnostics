Comprehensive Pulmonary Services at Sunrise Hospital

Sunrise Hospital is committed to offering a wide range of pulmonary care services. Using advanced diagnostic tools and tailored treatment plans, we ensure optimal care for all respiratory health needs.


1.Asthma Management
Asthma can limit your ability to enjoy life, but we provide effective solutions, such as:

  • Identifying and addressing asthma triggers.
  • Prescribing inhalers, biologics, and medications to manage symptoms.
  • Guiding patients on lifestyle adjustments to prevent flare-ups and maintain long-term relief.

2.COPD Care
Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) requires specialized attention. Our services include:

  • Bronchodilator therapy to improve breathing.
  • Medications to slow disease progression.
  • Personalized smoking cessation programs to enhance overall lung function.

3.Treatment for Respiratory Infections and Pneumonia
Respiratory infections like pneumonia can take a toll on your health. At Sunrise Hospital, we provide:

  • Accurate diagnosis using advanced testing.
  • Comprehensive treatment plans for quick recovery.
  • Long-term care to reduce recurrence and complications.

4.Sleep Apnea Diagnosis and Treatment
Sleep apnea can affect both your physical and mental well-being. Our approach includes:

  • Detailed testing to identify sleep apnea symptoms.
  • CPAP therapy and non-invasive treatment options.
  • Guidance to improve sleep quality and overall health.

5.Pulmonary Function Testing (PFT)
We use state-of-the-art diagnostic tools to evaluate lung function and detect conditions like:

  • Asthma.
  • COPD.
  • Pulmonary fibrosis.

6.Smoking Cessation Programs
Quitting smoking is one of the most effective ways to improve lung health. We provide:

  • Behavioral counseling and emotional support.
  • Medications to reduce withdrawal symptoms.
  • Strategies to help you maintain a smoke-free life.

7.Lung Cancer Screening and Care
Early detection is key to better outcomes in lung cancer treatment. Our services include:

  • Regular screenings for at-risk individuals.
  • Timely interventions using advanced therapies.
  • Individualized treatment plans for each patient.

8.Interventional Pulmonology
For complex respiratory conditions, we provide specialized procedures such as:

  • Bronchoscopy for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
  • Pleural effusion management to alleviate breathing difficulties.
  • Individualized treatment plans for each patient.
