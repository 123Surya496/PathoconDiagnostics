Pathocon Diagnostic

Established in 2001 by Dr. G.N. Keshava Murthy (MBBS, DCP), our diagnostic center in Rajajinagar is renowned as a trusted AIDS Diagnostic Center. Certified with NABL, TGS, and ISO 9001:2015, we ensure the highest standards of accuracy, reliability, and confidentiality in every test we conduct. Equipped with advanced technology and a dedicated team, we offer a comprehensive range of diagnostic services to meet diverse healthcare needs. With over two decades of experience, we remain committed to excellence, patient care, and precision, making us a dependable partner in your health journey.
